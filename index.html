Our Vision

To provide excellent and quality services to the full satisfaction of our client by offer-ing best professional engineering practice and sustainable solutions.

Our Mission

To provide the highest quality of service to our clients, ensure long-term profitability, increase shareholder value, provide career opportunities, and continuously support the betterment of the community.

Purpose

To be a leader in the logistics & general supplies by providing enhanced services, relationship and profitability

Our Strategy

We place the highest value on teamwork and mutual respect among our employees and our clients. Our success is the result of working diligently as a team to do each job right as well as maintaining a long-term perspective and integrity in the face of daily pressures while ensuring excellence and satisfaction in our product and service.

What We Do?

Gooa World Freighters Limited provides:

  • safe and reliable long and short Haulage
  • Bulk and bagged Warehousing
  • Clearing and Forwarding
  • Bulk Cargo Handling
  • Air Cargo Services etc..
for general and specialized cargo to a diverse industry segment like steel, cement, food and many more in the East African Region.

With our industry expertise, innovative technology and a modern approach to the real world of logistics, our customers are assured that their cargo is in safe hands. In addition to the logistics footprint , the company plans to expand its trade by focusing on various aspects including General Supplies, supply of building and construction materials among others.
